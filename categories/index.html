Categories group tools by use case. Everything runs in your browser with no signup. If you want full workflows (not just single tools), use the AI Hub.

How categories work

Pick a category, open a tool, run it, and copy or share the result. Share links encode your inputs in the URL, so you can revisit and tweak later.

  • Social / Creator for hooks, scripts, usernames, hashtags, and content ideas.
  • Writing for headlines, newsletters, LinkedIn posts, and outlines.
  • Career for resumes, STAR stories, outreach drafts, and freelance math.
  • Business / Finance for break-even, burn/runway, loan payoff, and deal analysis.
  • Repurpose for turning one input into multiple outputs and planning content.
