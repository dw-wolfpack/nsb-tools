What this page is

The AI Hub is a collection of playbooks, checklists, and a toolkit for using AI in job search, founder ops, engineering, and small business. No product pitches; just step-by-step workflows and guardrails you can run today.

Who it is for

Job seekers, solo founders, operators, and small businesses who want structured ways to use AI for outreach, resumes, vendor eval, meeting notes, RAG readiness, and adoption.

How to use this hub in 5 minutes

  1. Pick your track below (job seeker, founder, operator, small business).
  2. Open the linked playbook or checklist.
  3. Follow the steps; copy templates where given.
  4. Pair with the calculators and tools linked from each page.

How to use the AI Hub safely

Review outputs before using them. Do not paste sensitive data into external AI tools. Use the Vendor Eval Scorecard before signing any AI vendor. The LLM Safety Review helps you check for prompt injection, data leakage, and access controls. Treat AI outputs as drafts; edit and verify before publishing. Keep a log of what you tried and what worked. These playbooks and checklists are guardrails, not replacements for judgment.
