Copy and paste prompt pack

Tool-agnostic prompts. Do not paste sensitive or confidential data.

Fast summary

Use when you need a quick, neutral recap.

Summarize the following in 3 bullet points. Keep each under 25 words. Do not add opinions or recommendations.

Checklist from notes

Use when you want a step-by-step list.

Turn these notes into a checklist. One action per item. Use imperative verbs. Number the items.

Interview questions

Use with a job description to prep fast.

Generate 8 interview questions from this job description. Mix behavioral, situational, and role-specific. Focus on skills and fit.

Vendor evaluation

Use before you pilot or buy a tool.

Draft 10 vendor evaluation questions. Cover pricing, data handling, SLA, support, exit terms, and references.

Decision memo

Use when you need a clean recommendation.

Write a one-page decision memo. Sections: context, options (3 max), pros and cons, unknowns, recommendation. Be concise.

Next actions

Use after a meeting to create tasks.

Produce a next-actions list from this meeting. Format: action | owner | deadline. One line per item. No fluff.

Tip: paste your input under the prompt and ask for the format you want (email, checklist, table).

Guardrails

  • Do not paste sensitive or confidential data into any AI tool.
  • Ask for sources or quotes when the output is factual.
  • Verify numbers and assumptions before acting on them.
  • Keep a decision log (URLs, inputs, outputs) for important choices.
  • Start with the smallest workflow that delivers value.
  • Measure time saved or outcome improved so you know what works.
