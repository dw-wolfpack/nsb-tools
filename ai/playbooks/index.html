AI Playbooks

Step-by-step AI workflows for job search, founder ops, RAG readiness, and small business adoption. Templates included.

Last updated: February 27, 2026

What this page is

AI Playbooks are step-by-step workflows you can follow with any AI assistant. Each playbook includes templates, prompts, and links to related tools and checklists.

How to use this hub in 5 minutes

  1. Choose a playbook that matches your goal (job search, founder ops, RAG, or small business).
  2. Open the playbook page and read the steps.
  3. Copy the templates and prompts into your AI tool.
  4. Use the linked calculators and checklists where suggested.

How to pick a playbook

Match the playbook to your goal. Job search: use the Job Search AI Workflow for targeting and outreach, plus the Resume ATS Checklist before applying. Founder ops: use the Founder Ops AI Workflow for decision memos and weekly planning. Engineering teams: use the RAG Readiness Playbook for retrieval, chunking, and evaluation. Small business: use the Small Business AI Adoption playbook for pilots and SOPs. Each playbook links to related tools and checklists. Start with one, finish it, then add the next. Templates are copy-paste ready.
