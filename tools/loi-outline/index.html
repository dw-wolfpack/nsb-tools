Offer / LOI Outline Generator

Generate LOI outline bullets based on asset + price + financing structure.

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What this tool does

Generates a bullet-point outline for a Letter of Intent (LOI) or offer. You enter asset type, purchase price, financing structure, and number of bullets. Fill in the placeholders with your specifics before submitting. Not legal advice. Not AI-powered.

When to use it

  • Drafting an LOI or offer and need a structure fast
  • You have asset, price, and financing but not the bullet order
  • Want a checklist (price, earnest money, contingencies, diligence)

Inputs explained

  • Asset type / description – What you are buying (e.g. SaaS business, $2M ARR).
  • Purchase price – Price or range (e.g. $1.5M).
  • Financing structure – How you plan to pay (e.g. SBA 7a, cash, seller financing).
  • Number of bullets – How many outline bullets to generate.

How to use it

  1. Enter asset, price, financing. Set number of bullets. Click Generate.
  2. Edit placeholders with your details. Add or remove bullets as needed.

Common mistakes

  • Submitting without replacing placeholders with your specifics
  • Treating the outline as legally binding (LOIs are typically non-binding; consult an attorney)

Use it with AI

This tool is not AI-powered. You can use the results with an AI assistant in these ways:

  • Paste the outline and ask for full sentence drafts for each bullet.
  • Ask an AI to suggest 5 additional contingency or diligence bullets.

LOIs are typically non-binding; consult an attorney.

What bullets are included?

Asset, price, financing, earnest money, timeline, contingencies, diligence.
