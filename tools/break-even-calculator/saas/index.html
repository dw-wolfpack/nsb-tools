Break-even Calculator for SaaS

SaaS uses MRR and customers instead of units. Fixed costs (salaries, infra, tools), gross margin (revenue minus COGS), and ARPA (average revenue per account per month) determine break-even.

Typical inputs for SaaS

Fixed: salaries, cloud, tools, office. Gross margin 70 to 85% for software. ARPA: blend of plans if you have tiers.

Examples

Example 1: $12k fixed, 80% margin, $65 ARPA. Contribution $52/customer/mo. Break-even ~231 customers, ~$15k MRR.

Example 2: Early-stage: $8k burn, 75% margin, $40 ARPA. Break-even ~267 customers.

Example 3: Enterprise: higher ARPA ($500+) lowers customer count to break even.

FAQ

What is ARPA?

Average revenue per account per month. MRR divided by customer count.

Gross margin for SaaS?

Revenue minus hosting, support, payment fees. Typically 70 to 85%.
