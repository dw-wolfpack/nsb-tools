Break-even Calculator for Service Businesses

Service businesses (consulting, agencies, freelancing) have fixed costs (rent, salaries, tools) and variable cost per "unit" (e.g. per project or per hour delivered).

Typical inputs for service businesses

Fixed costs: rent, salaries, software, marketing. Variable: subcontractors, materials, travel per engagement. Price per unit: average project fee or blended hourly rate.

Examples

Example 1: $8k fixed, $15 variable per unit, $120 price. Contribution $105/unit. Break-even ~76 units or ~$9,150 revenue.

Example 2: Agency with $15k fixed, $50 variable (subcontractor), $200 project fee. Break-even ~100 projects.

Example 3: Freelancer: "unit" = billed hour. Fixed = living costs, variable = minimal.

FAQ

What is a "unit" for services?

Whatever you sell: project, retainer month, billed hour. Use your average.

Variable cost for pure services?

Often low (tools, subcontractors). Can be $0 if you deliver solo.

Is this financial advice?

No. Educational only.
