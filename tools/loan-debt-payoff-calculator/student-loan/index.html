Loan Payoff Calculator for Student Loans

Model payoff timeline and total interest for federal or private student loans. Add extra payments to see how much faster you pay off and how much interest you save.

Typical inputs for student loans

Federal rates vary; 4 to 7% is common. Private can be 5 to 12%. Principal $20k to $80k. Minimum payments depend on plan. Try adding $50 to $200 extra per month.

Examples

Example 1: $35k at 5.5%, $350/mo minimum. Payoff ~10 years, ~$11k interest. Add $100 extra: payoff ~7 years, save ~$3k interest.

Example 2: $50k at 6.8%, $450/mo. Payoff ~12 years. $150 extra knocks off ~3 years.

Example 3: Avalanche: put extra toward highest-rate loan first. Use calculator per loan.

FAQ

Does this work for income-driven plans?

It models fixed payments. Income-driven plans change over time; use as a ballpark.

Federal vs private?

Same math. Enter the specific loan's principal and rate.

Is this financial advice?

No. Educational only. Consult a financial advisor.
