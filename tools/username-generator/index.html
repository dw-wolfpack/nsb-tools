Username Generator

Generate platform-safe usernames based on niche, tone, keywords, and name.

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What this tool does

This tool generates platform-safe usernames (letters, numbers, underscores only) from your niche, optional keywords, and name. Outputs work on Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, and similar platforms. You get 3 to 20 options per run and can regenerate for more.

When to use it

  • Starting a new creator or brand account and need ideas
  • Rebranding and want options that fit your niche
  • Checking that a style of username is available (you still verify on the platform)
  • Getting unstuck when you cannot think of a memorable handle

Inputs explained

  • Niche – Your topic or focus (e.g. fitness, tech). Drives the word patterns.
  • Keywords – Optional. Comma-separated words to narrow results (e.g. coach, training).
  • Name – Optional. Part of your name to blend into usernames.
  • Number of usernames – How many to generate (3 to 20).

How to use it

  1. Enter your niche (required) and optional keywords or name.
  2. Set how many usernames you want (default 8).
  3. Click Generate.
  4. Use Regenerate for more options. Copy or save any you like.
  5. Check availability on the platform before committing.

Common mistakes

  • Using special characters or spaces in the niche field (the tool outputs only letters, numbers, underscores)
  • Assuming generated usernames are available (always verify on the platform)
  • Requesting too few options and missing better fits

Use it with AI

This tool is not AI-powered. It uses fixed rules and wordlists. You can use the results with an AI assistant in these ways:

  • Paste a few generated usernames and ask for variations in the same style.
  • Ask an AI to suggest which username fits your brand best given your niche and goals.
  • Use the AI Toolkit to combine this with other workflows (e.g. content ideas, hooks).

FAQ

Are these usernames available?

We do not check availability. Always verify on the platform before committing.

Why only letters, numbers, underscores?

Most platforms restrict usernames to these characters for consistency and compatibility.

How many can I generate at once?

The default is 8; you can request 3 to 20 per run.
