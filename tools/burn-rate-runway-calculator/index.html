Burn Rate / Runway Calculator

Calculate net burn, runway, and 12-month projection.

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What this tool does

Calculates burn rate (expenses minus revenue), runway in months, and a 12-month cash projection. Optional revenue growth rate. Educational only; not financial advice. Not AI-powered.

When to use it

  • Planning runway and need a simple burn and projection
  • Modeling what happens if revenue grows (or stays flat)
  • Preparing a board or investor update with cash metrics

Inputs explained

  • Cash on hand – Current cash balance.
  • Monthly revenue, monthly expenses – Current run rate.
  • Monthly revenue growth % – (Optional) Growth applied to revenue each month in the projection.

How to use it

  1. Enter cash, revenue, expenses. Optionally add revenue growth. Click Calculate.
  2. Review burn, runway, and 12-month projection. Use scenarios to compare.

Common mistakes

  • Using one-off revenue or expenses (use recurring run rate)
  • Assuming growth is linear (this tool applies simple monthly growth)
  • Forgetting to update when cash or burn changes (re-run when assumptions change)

Use it with AI

This tool is not AI-powered. It is a calculator. You can use the results with an AI assistant in these ways:

  • Paste the runway and projection and ask for a one-page summary of risks and options.
  • Ask an AI to suggest 5 levers to extend runway (cut costs, grow revenue, raise).
