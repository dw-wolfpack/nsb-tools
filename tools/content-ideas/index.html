Content Ideas Generator

Generate content ideas with angles, hooks, and format suggestions.

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What this tool does

Generates content ideas with angle, hook, and format suggestions (carousel, reel, thread, etc.) tailored to your niche. You set niche and number of ideas (3 to 12). Each idea includes a suggested format. Not AI-powered.

When to use it

  • Planning a content calendar and need a batch of ideas
  • Stuck on what to post next in your niche
  • Want format suggestions (reel vs carousel vs thread) per idea

Inputs explained

  • Niche – Your topic or focus (e.g. productivity). Drives the angles and hooks.
  • Number of ideas – How many ideas to generate (3 to 12).

How to use it

  1. Enter your niche. Set number of ideas. Click Generate.
  2. Browse angles and formats. Copy or export to CSV.
  3. Pick one, expand the hook, and create the content.

Common mistakes

  • Niche that is too broad so ideas feel generic
  • Requesting too few ideas and running out of options

Use it with AI

This tool is not AI-powered. You can use the results with an AI assistant in these ways:

  • Paste one idea and ask for 5 variations on the same angle.
  • Ask an AI to turn one idea into a full outline or script.
  • Use the AI Toolkit for content workflows.

FAQ

What is an angle?

The perspective or approach for the content (e.g. myth busting, step-by-step).

How do I use these ideas?

Pick one, expand the hook, and create the content in your chosen format.

Are ideas unique each time?

Yes. Regenerate for new combinations.
