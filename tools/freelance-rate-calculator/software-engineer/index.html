Freelance Rate Calculator for Software Engineers

Software engineers often command higher freelance rates due to specialized skills. This page provides defaults and guidance tailored to devs.

Typical inputs for software engineers

Common targets: desired after-tax $100k to $150k, annual expenses $20k to $40k, billable 25 to 30 hours per week, 2 to 4 weeks vacation, 65 to 75% utilization. Buffer 15 to 25% helps cover scope creep and unpaid discovery.

FAQ

What utilization do most software freelancers see?

65 to 75% is common. Non-billable time includes sales, admin, learning, and context switching.

Should I price by hour or project?

Both. Use hourly for T&M; use project minimums (10h, 20h, 40h) for fixed-scope to avoid underquoting short engagements.

How does this differ from a data engineer or designer rate?

Same formula. Adjust inputs for your niche. Data engineers and senior devs often sit in similar or higher ranges.
