Repurpose Pack Generator

Convert a source text into hooks + scripts + captions + visual ideas (heuristics only).

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What this tool does

Extracts key ideas from your source text and generates hooks, script beats, captions, and visual ideas for repurposing one piece into many. Paste a blog post or transcript (up to about 2000 characters for best results). Not AI-powered; uses heuristics.

When to use it

  • You have one long-form piece and want to turn it into short-form content
  • Need hooks, captions, and visual ideas from a single source
  • Repurposing a podcast or video transcript into clips and posts

How to use it

  1. Paste your source text. Click Generate.
  2. Review hooks, script, captions, and visual ideas. Use each for a different format.

Common mistakes

  • Pasting too much text (tool works best with a focused chunk)
  • Expecting fully written scripts (you get beats and ideas to expand)

Use it with AI

This tool is not AI-powered. You can use the results with an AI assistant in these ways:

  • Paste one hook and ask for 5 variations or a full 30-second script.
  • Ask an AI to turn the visual ideas into a shot list or storyboard.
