FAQ

Last updated: February 27, 2025

NSB Tools is a small set of fast tools that run in your browser. No signup. If you want workflows and checklists to go with the tools, start at the AI Hub.

Do I need to sign up?

No. All tools run in your browser. No account required.

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. Inputs and outputs stay on your device. We use localStorage only for preferences and recently used tools.

How does sharing work?

Most tools support share links that encode your inputs in the URL so you can reopen the same view later. If a page has a Share button, it is safe to copy and send.

Do you store my inputs?

No. NSB Tools does not store your inputs on a server. Some tools may save lightweight preferences locally in your browser (like last used category or recently used tools).

Can I use this for client work?

Yes. Outputs are drafts and numbers to help you move faster. Always sanity check anything high-stakes before you ship or send it.

Where should I start?

  • Browse categories to find tools by use case.
  • AI Toolkit for prompts and “best next tool” picks.
  • Glossary if you want quick definitions for common terms.

What is Pro?

Pro is a planned paid tier that will remove daily generation limits and unlock extra features. Not available yet.
