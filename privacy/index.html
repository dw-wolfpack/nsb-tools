Privacy

We do not sell your data. We do not track you for advertising.

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What we store

NSB Tools is built to run in your browser. Most usage never leaves your device.

localStorage

We use browser localStorage only for:

  • Recently used tools (to show links on the home page)
  • Saved outputs (when you choose to save)
  • Daily generation count (to enforce free-tier limits)
  • Pro flag (client-side only; no real auth yet)

All of this stays on your device. We never receive it.

What we do not do

  • No selling personal data
  • No ad tracking
  • No server-side storage of your tool inputs or outputs

If a tool has a share link, it encodes your inputs in the URL so you can reopen the same scenario later. You control whether you share that link.

Analytics

We may add optional analytics in the future to understand basic usage (for example, which pages are most helpful). If we do, we will update this page.
