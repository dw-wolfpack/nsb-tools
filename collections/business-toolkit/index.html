FAQ

Are these financial advice?

No. All calculators are educational estimators. Consult a financial advisor or tax professional for decisions.

Can I save and compare scenarios?

Yes. Free users can save up to 2 scenarios per tool and compare up to 2. Pro unlocks unlimited saves and compare up to 4.

Can I share or embed these?

Yes. Each tool includes share links (with prefilled params), citation snippets, and iframe embed code. See link to us for badges and snippets.

Do calculators use my data?

No. Everything runs client-side. No data leaves your device.
