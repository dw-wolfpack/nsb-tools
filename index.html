What this page is

NSB Tools is a free, client-side tool hub. Everything runs in your browser. No signup, no tracking. Generate copy, run calculators, and share results via URL.

Who it is for

Creators, writers, job seekers, founders, and operators who want fast drafts and numbers without creating an account.

How to use it in 5 minutes

  1. Pick a category or search by name.
  2. Open a tool and enter your inputs.
  3. Hit Generate or Calculate.
  4. Copy the output or share the link.
