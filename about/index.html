About NSB Tools

NSB Tools is a collection of fast, client-side tools for creators, writers, and professionals. Everything runs in your browser. No backend, no signup, no tracking.

Last updated: February 27, 2025

What it is

These are simple tools for getting unstuck and moving faster: writing drafts, quick calculators, and checklists that turn messy inputs into usable output.

Most tools are built to be copy-first. Generate, skim, tweak, paste, ship.

Who it is for

  • Creators who need hooks, scripts, captions, and content ideas
  • Job seekers who want cleaner resume bullets, STAR stories, and outreach drafts
  • Founders and operators who want fast math: break-even, runway, pricing, ROI

How it works

Tools run client-side. Inputs stay on your device. Some pages generate shareable links so you can save scenarios, compare options, or send a friend the same view.
